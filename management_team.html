About Fedironics iMeter

The iMeter manufactured by Fedironics Intelligent Metering Company is the first of its kind in Nigeria, and the first meter with a dedicated app in the world. This on its own is an unprecedented niche for the company, its product and services. Also our AMI solution of the company is ingeniously designed to take advantage of and suit our immediate environment and to solve indigenous problems while considering the readily available technology, cheap cost and reliable communication at hand i.e. the Global System for Mobile Communication (GSM), also the first of its kind in Nigeria and the World.
