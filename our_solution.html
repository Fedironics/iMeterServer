iMeter

The Intelligent Meter (iMeter), is an incredibly smart meter that measures energy, detects tamper, manages energy, provides prepayment service, manages tariff and logs events in near-real time while integrating a dedicated mobile application interfaces (MAI) the first of its kind in the world and a cellular network for the purpose of extensive remote management by the customers and utilities. The iMeter’s accuracy and reliability is within Class 0.5 (prototype) and built upon standard components that have met international standards and specifications in performance and safety.

OUR AMI SOLUTION

The Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) solution is simply an integrated system of smart meters, communication networks and data management systems that enables two-way communication between utilities and customers. This service is complementary to our product and it’s built over an already existing network so it required no extra cost on physical structure.

INTERNET OF THINGS (IoT):

The AMI together with the reliable iMeters’ network mesh provides IoT portals, channels and extended gateways for IoT user applications such as weather stations, tracking, mapping and location, remote water pump, remote fire alarm system for city fire services to mention but a few.

VALUE PROPOSITION

CUSTOMER I (Electricity distribution companies)

  • Cost: Affordable to purchase and maintain
  • Customer I user experience: Web portal, server tracking every event
  • Control energy subscription modes: Prepaid and postpaid
  • Revenue Management: Varying tariff or Time-Of-Use (TOU), Bill tariff and energy consumption charts and evaluation, energy time or top-up energy purchase and so on.
  • Revenue Protection: Secured Machine-to-Machine (M2M) Communication, 12 Energy theft prevention schemes with theft location identification, integrated service switch for automatic (prepaid mode) and manual (postpaid) power disconnection.
  • Meter Data management: Meter Number, Location, Meter type, Owner, Consumption etc
  • Maintenance, upgrade and planning: Energy demand control, field access and Inspection mobile/desktop App (wired & wireless), remote meter programming.
  • Employee management (Meter Installers/inspectors) and their operations
  • Customer Relation Management (CRM): Customer data management, complaint management, fault detection and response, power outage notification.

CUSTOMER II (Electricity Users e.g. Government Institutions, Industries, Banks, Residences etc)

  • Customer II user experience: Mobile App and Web portal, complaints/report faults, Meter with built-in Human Machine Interface (HMI) i.e. keypad & LCD, accurately measured bills rather than estimated bills.
  • Payment options: Postpaid and prepaid energy plan
  • Automatic change over
  • Customer smart connect/disconnect meter: Through Meter built-in HMI, Web portal and Mobile app
  • Energy management: Through budget setting and notification
  • Various payment channels: Meter built-in HMI, Web portal and Mobile app
